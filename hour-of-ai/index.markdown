    <h2>Overview</h2>

    <p>
      Hour of AI is being developed as a collaborative team project (Team #15),
      involving four members. The goal is to introduce fundamental ideas behind
      artificial intelligence in a clear, engaging, and beginner-friendly way.
    </p>

    <p>
      The project focuses on breaking down complex AI concepts into
      explanations, emphasizing real-world relevance rather than technical
      depth.
    </p>

    <h2>Current Status</h2>

    <p>
      The project is currently ongoing. Content development, structure, and
      presentation materials are actively being prepared, with final delivery
      expected by December 20.
    </p>

    <h2>What the Project Includes</h2>

    <ul class="project-list">
      <li>Introductory explanation of AI concepts</li>
      <li>Interactive and visual learning elements</li>
      <li>Team-based presentation</li>
      <li>Certification upon completion</li>
    </ul>
